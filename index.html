Xing Xing, who's name means "Twin Stars", is a Tibetan Macaque monkey who was rescued and now lives in Daliang Mountain in East China. She only has one arm due to a loss of the other when she was harmed by a wild boar trap. She was then sold to a circus after being found in this condition. Shorlty after she went on to live in a restaurant so she could entertain customers, but due to her large appetite she also had to leave the restaurant.

Xing Xing was lucky to come across an elderly Buddhist nun who the internet refers to as "Grandma" (as she is like Xing Xing's Grandmother) where she has lived ever since. So, Xing Xing is not just a charming and cute persona, she is the epitome of perserverance.
