All About Xing Xing the Monkey!

An Insight Into the Rescue Monkey From Instagram Who Became an Overnight Sensation

Who Is Xing Xing?

Xing Xing is a Tibetan Macaque monkey who was rescued and now lives in Daliang Mountain in East China. She only has one arm due to a loss of the other when she was harmed by a wild boar trap. She was then sold to a circus after being found in this condition. Shorlty after she went on to live in a restaurant so she could entertain customers but due to her large appetite she also had to leave the restaurant.

Xing Xing was lucky to come across an elderly Buddhist nun who the internet refers to as "Grandma" (as she is like Xing Xing's Grandmother) where she has lived ever since. So, Xing Xing is not just a charming and cute perona, she is the epitomy of perserverance.

Xing Xing on the Internet:

The entire reason we know Xing Xing's story is because of her instagram. Videos of Xing Xing spending time with Grandma, eating food and playing with her cat have circulated the internet for months going viral. She has grown such a big internet presence and following that she now has fans from across the world venture to visit her daily. She is known for getting a bit feisty with some, but she is beloved nonetheless. In fact, one fan (Aka my mother) in particular stated, "Xing Xing keeps me going".

What Does Xing Xing Like to Do?

    1.Xing Xing is friends with a cat that also lives around the mountains. They can often be found playing and cuddling with each other.
    2. Xing Xing loves to eat. Some of her most famous videos are of her being presented food from her fans where she proceeds to sniff them, lick them and look at them and then throw them in disgust.
